Your Festival Week

Suggested itinerary for the ultimate Summer Tides experience

1
JUL
✈️ Arrive in Malindi
Fly into Malindi Airport (direct from Nairobi, 1hr). Check into accommodation. Beach time. Pre-party dinner with the crew. Early night to rest up.
2
JUL
🎵 Festival Day 1
Beach day, pool time at your hotel. Afternoon nap is mandatory. Festival gates open evening. Opening night sets. Pace yourself - it's a marathon not a sprint!
3
JUL
🌅 Festival Day 2 (Main Day)
Sleep in. Beach recovery. Pool. The main headliners tonight. This is THE night. Catch the legendary sunrise set as the sun comes up over the ocean.
4
JUL
🎪 Festival Day 3 (Closing)
Final day vibes. Closing sets. The emotional farewell. Last dance on the beach. After-party until sunrise (or until you can't anymore).
5
JUL
🏖️ Recovery Day
Late checkout if possible. Spa day. Beach recovery. Reflect on the memories. Evening flight home or extend for more coast time (Watamu whale sharks are 30 min away!).
