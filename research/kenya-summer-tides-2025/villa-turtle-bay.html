🏨 Property Overview

Turtle Bay Beach Club is the diver's and eco-traveler's choice in Watamu. This award-winning eco-resort sits directly on the boundary of Watamu Marine National Park, giving you instant access to some of Kenya's best snorkeling and diving sites.

The resort takes sustainability seriously – solar power, water recycling, and a long-running partnership with Local Ocean Conservation for turtle protection. You're not just visiting; you're supporting marine conservation.

With an all-inclusive rate that includes meals, drinks, snorkeling gear, and kayaks, Turtle Bay offers exceptional value. The on-site dive center arranges whale shark excursions daily during season, and you can literally walk to the Marine Park jetty.

🌟 What Makes This Location Unforgettable

Step off the beach and into a coral wonderland. Turtle Bay's location on the Marine Park boundary means world-class snorkeling is steps away – no boat needed. Combine this with the lowest whale shark trip logistics of any resort (the boats leave from the neighboring jetty) and you have the ultimate marine adventure base. Plus, every stay supports real conservation work.
