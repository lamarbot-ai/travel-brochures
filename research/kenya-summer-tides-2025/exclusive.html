💡 Insider Tips

What the locals know

Best Whale Shark Timing
Go out at first light (6 AM). By 9 AM, there can be 10+ boats around each shark. Early boats often get 30+ minutes of solo time with the giants.
Money-Saving Hack
Book villas through local agents like Safari Doctors (+254 722 203636) instead of Airbnb – often 30% cheaper and better service.
Best Local Eats
Skip hotel restaurants for dinner at Savannah (Watamu) or Ali Barbour's Cave (Diani). For street food, the nyama choma stands near Malindi market are unbeatable.
Lamu Pro Tip
Fly to Lamu – the road is long and checkpoints slow. Kenya Airways and Fly540 have daily flights from Malindi (20 min) and Nairobi (1 hr).
Tide Timing
Check tides before booking beach activities. Low tide exposes rock pools for exploring, but swimming is better at high tide. Apps like "Tide Charts" work well here.
Respect Local Culture
The coast is predominantly Muslim. Dress modestly when visiting villages or Lamu. Swimwear is fine at resort beaches only.

Need Help Planning?

Local operators often don't have websites. WhatsApp is king here.
