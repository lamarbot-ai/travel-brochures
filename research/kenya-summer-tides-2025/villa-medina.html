🏨 Property Overview

Medina Palms offers the ultimate group experience on Kenya's coast – stunning private residences ranging from 1 to 4 bedrooms, each with its own private pool. These aren't hotel rooms; they're fully-equipped luxury homes where you can spread out, cook together, and enjoy complete privacy.

The architecture blends Swahili tradition with contemporary luxury. Hand-carved Lamu doors, makuti-thatched roofs, and local coral stone create an authentic coastal atmosphere, while modern amenities ensure every comfort. Each villa comes with a dedicated housekeeper and optional private chef service.

Located just 5 minutes from Watamu's whale shark launching points, Medina Palms is the perfect base for marine adventures. The beach club offers water sports, a stunning infinity pool, and excellent dining with your feet in the sand.
