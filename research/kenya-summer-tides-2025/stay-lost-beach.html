🏨 Property Overview

Q Boutique Resort is the highest-rated property in Malindi (9.4/10 on Booking.com) and the perfect base for Summer Tides Festival. The resort offers a stunning pool, lush gardens, and the kind of peace and quiet you'll desperately need after nights of dancing.

Located just 15 minutes from Lost Beach Club, you're close enough to pop back for a nap between sets, but far enough for actual rest. The breakfast is included and consistently praised in reviews - fuel up before festival nights.

The Executive Rooms with balconies are perfect for groups, with space to spread out and plan your festival fits. Staff can arrange transport to/from the festival venue.
