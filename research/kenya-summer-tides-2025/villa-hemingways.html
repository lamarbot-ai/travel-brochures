🏨 Villa Overview

Named after Ernest Hemingway, who famously fished these waters in the 1930s, Hemingways Watamu stands as Kenya's premier luxury coastal retreat. The resort seamlessly blends colonial elegance with contemporary comfort, offering unobstructed views of the Indian Ocean and direct access to Watamu Marine National Park.


Each of the 74 rooms features handcrafted furniture, private balconies, and the legendary Hemingways service. The on-site PADI dive center runs daily whale shark excursions during season (July-September), with a 90%+ success rate. The resort's speedboats can have you swimming alongside gentle giants within 30 minutes of departure.

🌟 What Makes This Location Unforgettable

Wake up to dolphin pods playing in the bay. Spend your morning swimming alongside the world's largest fish. Return to sundowners on your private balcony as dhows sail past against the sunset. Hemingways isn't just a hotel – it's the gateway to Kenya's most extraordinary marine encounters, wrapped in the kind of service that made it Hemingway's choice 90 years ago.
