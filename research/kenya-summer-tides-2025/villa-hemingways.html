🏨 Property Overview

Named after Ernest Hemingway, who famously fished these waters in the 1930s, Hemingways Watamu stands as Kenya's premier luxury coastal retreat. The resort seamlessly blends colonial elegance with contemporary comfort, offering unobstructed views of the Indian Ocean and direct access to Watamu Marine National Park.

Each of the 74 rooms features handcrafted Lamu furniture, private ocean-facing balconies, and the legendary Hemingways service that has earned it multiple awards. The on-site PADI 5-Star dive center runs daily whale shark excursions during season (July-September), with a remarkable 90%+ encounter rate.

The resort's speedboats can have you swimming alongside gentle giants within 30 minutes of departure. After your ocean adventures, retreat to the award-winning spa or enjoy sundowners at the iconic waterfront bar.

🌟 What Makes This Location Unforgettable

Wake up to dolphin pods playing in the bay. Spend your morning swimming alongside the world's largest fish – the magnificent whale shark. Return to sundowners on your private balcony as traditional dhows sail past against a blazing sunset. Hemingways isn't just a hotel – it's the gateway to Kenya's most extraordinary marine encounters, wrapped in the kind of timeless service that made it Hemingway's choice nearly a century ago.
