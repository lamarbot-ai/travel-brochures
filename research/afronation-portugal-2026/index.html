8-Day Festival Week Itinerary

The complete Afronation experience: pre-parties → festival → recovery

1
MON

June 29 - Arrival Day

  • Fly into Faro Airport (FAO) - 1 hour drive to Portimão
  • Check into accommodation, settle in
  • Beach walk at Praia da Rocha - scout the festival venue
  • Chill dinner at Alvor Marina - fresh seafood
  • Early night to recover from travel
2
TUE

June 30 - Welcome Parties Begin 🎊

  • Morning beach time at Praia da Rocha
  • Welcome Pool Party at NoSoloÁgua (2pm-10pm)
  • Meet fellow festival-goers, network
  • Dinner at Restaurante Squash
  • First night out in Portimão
3
WED

July 1 - Day Party Vibes 🎉

  • Sleep in, late brunch
  • Hotel pool parties & artist meet-and-greets
  • Rooftop brunch experience
  • Beach club afternoon
  • Light dinner, pace yourself!
4
THU

July 2 - Adventure Day ⛵

  • Boat party & Benagil Cave tour (morning)
  • Swim in crystal-clear grottos
  • Rest up in afternoon - big days ahead!
  • All White Pool Party preview events
  • Early dinner, get outfit ready for Day 1
5
FRI

July 3 - FESTIVAL DAY 1 🎵

  • Late morning - hydrate, eat well
  • Quick beach dip to get energized
  • Festival gates open (check schedule)
  • Burna Boy, Asake, Tyla and more!
  • Dance until late under the stars
6
SAT

July 4 - FESTIVAL DAY 2 🎵

  • Sleep in as late as possible
  • Beach recovery, pool time
  • Day party between sets (if energy permits)
  • Festival Day 2 - Main headliners
  • After parties around Portimão
7
SUN

July 5 - FESTIVAL DAY 3 🎵

  • Last day - give it everything!
  • Beach morning to soak it all in
  • Festival finale - closing performances
  • Wizkid, Gunna, Kehlani headline
  • Official closing party celebrations
8
MON

July 6 - Recovery & Departure

  • Late checkout if possible
  • Farewell brunch at Vista Restaurante
  • Last beach walk at Praia da Rocha
  • Depart from Faro Airport (FAO)
  • Already planning for Afronation 2027! 🇵🇹
