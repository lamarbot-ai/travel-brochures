🏡 Villa Overview

Villa Vinati is a stunning tropical 2-bedroom villa nestled in the peaceful Kayu Tulang area of Canggu, surrounded by lush rice paddies. Perfect for remote workers, it features 100Mbps high-speed WiFi, a dedicated workspace, and a private pool you can enjoy 24/7.

The villa is run by Superhost Elizabeth (103 reviews, responds within 1 hour) and is part of the ecoBali partnership, making it an environmentally conscious choice. The master suite features a private balcony and gorgeous terrazzo bathtub.

🌾 What Makes This Location Unforgettable

Wake up to the sound of birds and the gentle rustle of rice paddies. Work from your private office with fiber internet while overlooking tropical greenery. Take a midday swim in your private pool, then scooter 10 minutes to some of Bali's best beaches and cafes. This is the perfect balance of productive work life and tropical relaxation.

  • Morning Sunrise at Batu Bolong Beach + surfing lesson (~$30 for 2 hours)
