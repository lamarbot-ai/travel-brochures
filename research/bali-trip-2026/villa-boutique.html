Free & Budget Activities

Campuhan Ridge Walk

Free! Beautiful ridge walk between two valleys. Best at sunrise. 2km easy walk with stunning views.

Ubud Palace & Market

Free entry to palace grounds. Art Market great for bargain hunting. Go early for best prices.

Sacred Monkey Forest

Entry ~$5. Hundreds of monkeys in ancient temple setting. Keep belongings secure!

Balinese Massage

Budget spas from $10-20 for 1-hour massage. Quality varies - ask locals for recommendations.
