Activities & Experiences

Lempuyang Temple "Gates of Heaven"

The famous Instagram spot with Mt. Agung framed in temple gates. Go at 6am to beat crowds.

Amed Snorkeling/Diving

Crystal clear waters, Japanese WWII shipwreck, coral gardens. One of Bali's best dive sites.

Tirta Gangga Water Palace

Royal water gardens with koi-filled pools. Swim in the sacred springs (bring swimsuit!).

Mt. Agung Sunrise Trek

Challenging but rewarding climb to Bali's highest peak. Book through local guides (4-8 hours).
