## Priority Tiers

### 🔴 CRITICAL (Must Have)
1. Private pool
2. Nice outdoor area (terrace, garden, views)
3. Peaceful/relaxing surroundings
4. 2+ bedrooms, 2 bathrooms
5. Fast, reliable WiFi for remote work

### 🟡 IMPORTANT (Strong Preference)
1. Dedicated workspace or good table setup
2. Under $3,500/month (ideally under $2,500)
3. 4.5+ star rating with multiple reviews
4. Full kitchen for long-term stay
5. AC in all rooms

### 🟢 NICE TO HAVE
1. Rice paddy or nature views
2. Near restaurants/cafes
3. Close to Ubud or Canggu cultural scene
4. Hot tub / outdoor bathtub
5. Beach within 30 min drive

### ⚪ DON'T CARE
1. Nightlife proximity
2. Gym/fitness
3. TV/entertainment
4. Parking (using scooter)
5. Daily housekeeping

## Scoring Methodology

**Total Weight: 100 points**

| Category | Weight | Scoring Criteria |
|----------|--------|------------------|
| **Location/Surroundings** | 25 | Views, peace, vibe (5=rice paddies/jungle, 1=noisy/urban) |
| **Amenities** | 25 | Pool quality, outdoor space, WiFi, workspace |
| **Value** | 25 | Price vs what you get ($/month ÷ quality) |
| **Reviews** | 15 | Rating × review count (4.9+ w/ 50+ reviews = max) |
| **Convenience** | 10 | Restaurants nearby, transport, distance to attractions |

### Scoring Formula
```
SCORE = (Location × 0.25) + (Amenities × 0.25) + (Value × 0.25) + (Reviews × 0.15) + (Convenience × 0.10)
```
