Hidden above Dahana, Seminyak 7pm-1am Mon-Sat

Hidden cocktail bar with secret entrance. Japanese minimalism meets jazz-inspired cocktails. Try "So What" (coffee/spice) or "Kind of Blue" (matcha/whisky). Ingredients from their own mountain garden.

🏔️ HIGHLAND ESCAPE

Hiland 1280

Bedugul (1,280m elevation!) Cool mountain climate

Escape the beach heat! Mountain lodge restaurant with fireplace, overlooking misty peaks. Leather armchairs, timber beams, golf course views. Try Osso Bucco or Crispy Pork Belly.

Mains $20-50

📍 How to Get There

1.5 hours from Canggu. Perfect day trip - combine with Ulun Danu Temple visit. Dress warm (it's actually cool up there!).
