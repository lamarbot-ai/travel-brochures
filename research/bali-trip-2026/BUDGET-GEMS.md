## 📍 AREA BREAKDOWN

### **AMED (Best Value)**
- 🌊 East Bali, quiet fishing village
- ⏱️ 2.5-3 hours from Ngurah Rai Airport
- 💰 Cheapest private pool villas in Bali
- 🤿 World-class snorkeling & diving (Japanese Shipwreck, Liberty Wreck at Tulamben)
- ⛰️ Views of Mount Agung
- 📶 WiFi: Generally good, 20-50 Mbps
- 🍜 Food: Local warungs, fresh seafood
- ⚠️ Trade-off: Limited nightlife, remote

### **LOVINA (Beach Lovers)**
- 🐬 Famous for dolphin watching
- 🏖️ Black sand beaches, calm waters
- ⏱️ 2.5-3 hours from airport
- 💰 Very affordable, less touristy than south
- 🌅 Spectacular sunsets
- 📶 WiFi: 15-40 Mbps typical
- ⚠️ Trade-off: Far from Ubud/Seminyak scene

### **MUNDUK (Mountain Retreat)**
- 🏔️ Highland area, cool climate (sweater weather!)
- 💧 Waterfalls (Twin Lakes, Munduk Waterfall)
- 🌿 Coffee & clove plantations
- ⏱️ 2 hours from airport
- 💰 Very budget-friendly
- ⚠️ Trade-off: No beaches, can be cloudy, limited pool options due to cooler climate

### **TABANAN (Authentic Bali)**
- 🌾 Real Balinese agriculture
- 🏛️ Tanah Lot Temple nearby
- ⏱️ 1-1.5 hours from airport
- 💰 Good value, less developed
- 🏄 Access to Kedungu surf beach
- ⚠️ Trade-off: Less infrastructure, fewer restaurants

## 💡 NEGOTIATION TIPS

1. **Always ask for monthly rates** - usually 30-50% discount vs daily
2. **Book direct when possible** - saves Airbnb's 15% fee
3. **Offer 3+ month stays** - deeper discounts
4. **Low season (Jan-Mar, Oct-Nov)** - negotiate harder
5. **Join Facebook groups** - find owner-direct deals
6. **Pay in IDR** - sometimes better rates than USD
