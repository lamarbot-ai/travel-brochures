## 💰 Budget Hacks (All Areas)

### Massage & Spa
- **Local warungs often know massage therapists** - $8-15/hour vs $60+ at spas
- **In-villa massage** - Ask host to arrange, $10-20/hour
- **Avoid "tourist price"** - Walk 2 blocks from main roads

### Food
- **Warungs** - Local restaurants, $2-5 per meal, delicious
- **Nasi campur** - Mixed rice plate, $2-3, filling
- **Babi guling** - Roast pig (if non-halal), $5-8, iconic
- **Cook at villa** - Markets are cheap, $10/day for groceries

### Transport
- **Rent scooter** - $70-100/month vs $30/day for driver
- **Grab/Gojek** - Local Uber, way cheaper than taxis
- **Share drivers** - Split day trips with other travelers

### Activities
- **Temples** - Most are $3-5 entry, sarongs provided
- **Beaches** - All public, free access (clubs charge for sunbeds)
- **Sunrise hikes** - Skip paid guides for popular trails
- **Cooking classes** - Book direct, not through hotels

## 📞 Booking Tips

1. **WhatsApp is king** - Most businesses prefer WhatsApp over email
2. **Book 2-3 days ahead** - Especially for private chefs & popular spas
3. **Cash discounts** - Many places offer 5-10% off for cash
4. **Ask your villa host** - They have connections and can negotiate
5. **Instagram DM** - Many services only book via Instagram
